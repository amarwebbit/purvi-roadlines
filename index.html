About Purvi Roadlines

Our Journey. Your Trust.

We are more than a brokerage. We are a team of logistics passionate about building a better transportation ecosystem, starting right here in Kanpur.

Our Story

Founded in [Year, e.g., 2018] in the industrial heart of Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, Purvi Roadlines started with a simple observation: the local logistics market was fragmented and inefficient. Businesses struggled to find reliable trucks, and truck owners wasted time and fuel looking for their next load.

We built this company from the ground up, forging one relationship at a time. We spent years on the ground, understanding the challenges of both shippers and transporters. This website is the next step in our evolution, bringing our on-the-ground experience to a digital platform to serve you better.

Our Mission in Kanpur

To revolutionize logistics in Kanpur by providing a seamless, transparent, and technology-driven brokerage platform. We aim to empower local businesses and truck owners, making Kanpur a more efficient and profitable logistics hub.

Our Vision

To be Northern India's most trusted and sought-after logistics partner, expanding from our strong Kanpur roots to connect every city, town, and village across the nation with reliability and integrity.
